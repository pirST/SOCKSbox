## Privacy Policy for SOCKSbox
Last Updated: January 26, 2026
This Privacy Policy describes how SOCKSbox ("the Extension") handles user information.
1. No Data Collection
We believe in your right to privacy. SOCKSbox does not collect, store, use, or share any personal data, browsing history, or user information. All settings and configurations remain strictly local on your device. No data is transmitted from your device to any external servers.
2. Permissions
The Extension requests only the necessary browser permissions required for its functionality (such as managing proxy settings). These permissions are used exclusively to perform technical tasks on your device and do not involve the collection or monitoring of personal information.
3. Third-Party Services
The Extension does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
4. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and the "Last Updated" date will be updated accordingly.
